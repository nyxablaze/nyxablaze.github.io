EDC

my everyday carry is not necessarily the lightest carry - i tend to make sure i have absolutely everything i need. however i don't mind the weight, and using a messenger bag means there's only so much i can carry anyway giving me a weight constraint to make sure that no matter how much i put in the bag, it is not a struggle to carry around.
i do need to be mindful, as for the next few months i will be riding my bike to the train station. while not far at all, only about a kilometre, the weight of my bag can be an issue if the bag isn't balanced on my back. if, for example, the bag slips around to my side, i can throw my weight balance of a little bit but honestly it's not that hard to compensate for.

in my bag i carry:

• Acer Aspire 3 (A315-23-R7GR)
• UWA Notebook
• Officeworks A6 Notebook
• Kindle (10th gen)
• Samsung tablet s3
• General items (deoderant, lunch, tissues etc)
• Display file for paper (A4)
• Pencilcase

In my pencilcase i carry:

• Stylus for tablet ($20 aftermarket officeworks)
• Tombow Mono Graph Blue
• Non-specialised stationary (ruler, highlighters, erasers, spare pens)

on my person i carry

• Opel Mobile TouchFlip
• Jabra Elite 85t earbuds
• iPod touch 5th gen with apple wired earbuds (doesn't seem to work with bluetooth earbuds)
• Lucid wallet (from City Beach surf shops)


that sums up my EDC, i probably won't update it often unless i get a major update to it e.g. a new phone or a new device
